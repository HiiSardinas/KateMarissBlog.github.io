About Social Work

About
Social Work is a profession that primarily concerned with organized social service activity aimed to facilitate and strengthen basic social relationships and mutual adjustments between individuals and their social environment for the good of the individual and society

The best part of social work is knowing that you’ve made even the smallest difference in someone’s life. Social work is more than just a job, it’s a way of life. A good social worker has the ability to change and save lives.

If you want to Being part of something bigger than yourself, helping people improve their lives and Learn new skills, social work is the best suited for you.
